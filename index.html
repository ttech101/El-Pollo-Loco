The chickens can be killed by jumping on their heads or by throwing them with a bottle.
In a Mexican village named Salsaville, the salsa dancer Pepe is attacked by mutated chickens, known as "Chickenzillas." Armed with his salsa bottles, Pepe must navigate through various 2D platform levels, defeating the Chickenzillas. Along the way, he collects different salsa bottles and power-ups. Players must use their platforming skills to safely guide Pepe through the levels and save the village. "Pepe's Salsa Rescue" is an exciting jump-and-run game with a touch of salsa and Mexican flair. Pepe utilizes his salsa skills to defeat the Chickenzillas and restore peace to Salsaville.
